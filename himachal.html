Next Trip - 16th Feb

Day 1: Arrival in Shimla

  • Arrive in Shimla, the capital city of Himachal Pradesh.
  • Check into your hotel and freshen up.
  • Explore Mall Road in the evening, visit The Ridge, and enjoy the charming atmosphere.
  • Dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Shimla Sightseeing

  • Visit Jakhu Temple in the morning for panoramic views.
  • Explore Christ Church and Scandal Point.
  • Afternoon visit to Kufri for adventure activities and beautiful landscapes.
  • Return to Shimla for the night.

Day 3: Shimla to Manali

  • Check out from Shimla and travel to Manali (approximately 7 hours).
  • Arrive in Manali, check into your hotel, and relax.
  • Explore Old Manali and enjoy local cuisine in the evening.
  • Stroll around the Mall Road.

Day 4: Manali Adventure

  • Visit Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding and zorbing.
  • Explore the Hadimba Devi Temple and Manu Temple.
  • Relax at the Vashisht hot springs.
  • Evening at leisure in Manali.

Day 5: Manali to Dharamshala

  • Check out from Manali and travel to Dharamshala (approximately 7-8 hours).
  • Visit the Dalai Lama Temple and the Tibetan Museum.
  • Explore McLeod Ganj and enjoy the Tibetan culture.
  • Check into your hotel in Dharamshala.

Day 6: Dharamshala to Dalhousie

  • Check out from Dharamshala and travel to Dalhousie (approximately 4-5 hours).
  • Visit Khajjiar, often referred to as "Mini Switzerland."
  • Explore the Chamera Lake and enjoy the scenic beauty.
  • Check into your hotel in Dalhousie.

Day 7: Departure

  • Explore Dalhousie in the morning.
  • Visit the Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary or Dainkund Peak.
  • Depart from Dalhousie for your onward journey.
