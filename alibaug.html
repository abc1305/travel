Next Trip - 26th Jan

DAY 1

  • Explore Alibaug Beach: Begin your trip by spending time at Alibaug Beach. Relax, take a walk, or engage in beach activities.
  • Visit Kolaba Fort: Take a boat ride to Kolaba Fort and explore this historic structure with stunning views of the sea.
  • Local Markets: Wander through the local markets to discover unique items and soak in the local atmosphere.
  • Dine by the Beach: Enjoy local coastal cuisine at a beachside restaurant or shack.

DAY 2

  • Varsoli Beach: Start your day with a visit to Varsoli Beach. Enjoy the serene environment and maybe try some water sports.
  • Kihim Beach: Explore Kihim Beach, known for its natural beauty and tranquility.
  • Murud-Janjira Fort: Take a boat excursion to Murud-Janjira Fort and learn about its history.
  • Shopping and Relaxation: Spend your evening shopping for souvenirs or relaxing by the beach.
  • Dine by the Sea: Have dinner at a beachside restaurant, enjoying the sea breeze.

DAY 3

  • Have Breakfast by the sea view.
  • Accomodate your luggage and depart for your destinations.
